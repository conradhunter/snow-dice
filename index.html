How to play...

  1. Click a difficulty to generate a trick
  2. For 2 or more players
  3. If you land the trick you advance
  4. If you don't land the trick you suffer a forfeit or the player is out
  5. Players can bet money per trick
  6. The players can choose the amount of attempts allowed per trick
  7. Can be played in a SLVSH style format
